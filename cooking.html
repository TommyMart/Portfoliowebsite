Cooking

Since Covid, my skills in the kitchen have definitely improved, and so has my enjoyment and passion for finding new recipes that include home grown produce. Initially, I look for what produce in the garden is ready to consume, then jump on the computer to find recipes that include as many of the ingredients as possible. This is a fantastic way to save money, discover new recipes and avoid food wastage. Using this method I have stumbled across some excellent new recipes, that I will definitely be making next season!
