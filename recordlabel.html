Record Label

After spending several years immersed in music production, a desire for full control over the creative process from start to finish was realised. This drive inspired me to launch my own record label, a venture made possible with the support of two European-based distribution companies. Through this endeavour, my label has proudly released vinyl records by a select group of adored local and international artists, playing a significant role in my musical journey to date.
